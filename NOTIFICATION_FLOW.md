# Notification Flow - Simple Guide

## EMERGENCY REQUESTS

When customer submits emergency request and pays
- Customer will be notified
- Office Admin will be notified

When office admin assigns supervisor to emergency request
- Supervisor will be notified

When supervisor moves status to Quotation Stage
- Customer will be notified
- All Estimators will be notified

When supervisor moves status to Job Approved
- Supervisor will be notified
- Customer will be notified

When supervisor moves status to In Progress
- Customer will be notified
- Supervisor will be notified

When supervisor closes the emergency request
- Customer will be notified
- Office Admin will be notified

When supervisor cancels the emergency request
- Customer will be notified
- Office Admin will be notified

---

## CUSTOMER REQUESTS

When customer creates a request and supervisor is assigned
- Supervisor will be notified
- Supervisor's Manager will be notified (email)
- Customer will be notified (email)

When request is approved
- Customer will be notified
- Supervisor will be notified

When request is rejected
- Customer will be notified
- Supervisor will be notified

When request moved to Quotation Stage
- Customer will be notified
- All Estimators will be notified

When quotation is approved and work begins
- Supervisor will be notified
- Customer will be notified

When request is cancelled
- Customer will be notified
- Office Admin will be notified

When request is completed
- Customer will be notified
- Office Admin will be notified

---

## INTERNAL TASKS & ISSUES

When office admin or supervisor creates a new internal task
- Supervisor/Employee will be notified

When task moved to Quotation Stage
- All Estimators will be notified
- (Customer is NOT notified)

When task quotation is approved
- Supervisor will be notified
- (Customer is NOT notified)

When internal task is cancelled
- Office Admin will be notified
- (Customer is NOT notified)

When internal task is completed
- Office Admin will be notified
- (Customer is NOT notified)
